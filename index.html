Hello There!

I am Piyus Mohanty an aspiring Bioinformatics data scientist. I am currently based in Atlanta and pursuing a Master's degree in Bioinformatics at Georgia Tech.

I have been in love with the application of Computer science in the field of Biology and every step I have taken is in that direction. I have had more than 5 years of experience in dealing with Biological datasets (Bioinformatics based, Biomedical based, Biostats based, et). I am well versed with Machine Learning as well as NGS based applications (WES, WGS).

In addition to this, I have a book chapter on machine learning applications in translational bioinformatics that I published, and another project called ePFR (an improved hypoxemia detection measure which has high construct valididty and availability) is about to be published.
